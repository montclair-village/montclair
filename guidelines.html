Visitor Guidelines

To ensure that Montclair’s natural heritage remains pristine and accessible for future generations, we ask that all visitors adhere to the following guidelines:

Rules for Preserving Natural Spaces

  • Do not engage in unauthorized activities near the ravine, lava flow, or other protected natural areas.
  • Stay on designated trails and observation decks to avoid damaging fragile ecosystems.
  • Refrain from collecting or disturbing any natural artifacts, plants, or wildlife.
  • Please dispose of all trash in the provided bins and carry out what you bring in.

Safety Recommendations

  • Keep a safe distance from areas with active lava flows or unstable surfaces.

Thank you for helping preserve the natural beauty of Montclair and ensuring a safe experience for everyone.
