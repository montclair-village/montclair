Community Projects

Future Development Plans

Montclair is on the cusp of exciting changes that will enhance our village’s livability and community spirit. We have several plans in the pipeline, including:

  • Building Housing: New residential projects aimed at providing affordable and sustainable living spaces for our growing community.
  • Improving Paths and Public Spaces: Upgrading existing pathways and creating new green spaces to encourage walking, cycling, and community gatherings.
  • Enhancing Public Facilities: Renovating community centers and recreational areas to better serve the needs of residents.
