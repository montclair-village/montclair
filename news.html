News & Events

Townhall Renovation Update

February 2, 2025

The renovation of the townhall's main hall is progressing well. Work is scheduled to continue throughout the month, with a reopening celebration planned for mid April. Residents can expect improved facilities and updated community spaces.

New Partnership with Molossia Announced

May 1, 2025

Montclair is excited to announce a new alliance with Molossia, a micronation recognized for its independent spirit and cultural heritage. This partnership will foster collaborative projects and the exchange of innovative ideas in community planning and heritage preservation. Stay tuned for upcoming joint events and initiatives!
