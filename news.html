News & Events

Community Garden Project Launch

April 10, 2025

Montclair is excited to announce the launch of our new community garden project. Volunteers are welcome to join us in cultivating fresh produce and beautifying our communal spaces. The project will kick off next Saturday at the designated area near the townhall.

Townhall Renovation Update

March 28, 2025

The renovation of the townhall's main hall is progressing well. Work is scheduled to continue throughout the month, with a reopening celebration planned for early May. Residents can expect improved facilities and updated community spaces.

Upcoming Annual Festival

March 15, 2025

Mark your calendars for the annual Montclair Festival! This year, festivities will feature live music, local food vendors, guided historical tours, and family-friendly activities. The festival is set to take place on the first Saturday of June.
